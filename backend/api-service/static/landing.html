What you get

Prepare

Create a role with job title and description, attach your CV (PDF/DOCX/TXT). Everything is stored so you can reuse it across mock and live sessions.

ATS insight

See how well your CV aligns with the posting — strengths, gaps, and a simple fit score before you apply.

Mock interview

Technical or HR-style questions tailored to the job. Answer in writing or by voice, then request structured feedback.

Live interview

Ask out loud or type; your question is transcribed and answered in Situation · Task · Action · Result format, streamed as it’s generated.

History & attempts

Review past Q&A, compare practice runs, and track multi-question attempts with scores and summaries.

Desktop app

Prefer a native app? Use the Electron client from the repo — same flows, pointed at this API (/app uses the browser).

How the full process works

  1. Prepare — Add your target role, paste the job description, upload your CV.
  2. ATS — Run analysis to understand fit and keywords.
  3. Mock — Practice questions; get feedback on your answers.
  4. Live — Use voice or text during a real interview prep session; STAR answers stream back.
  5. History — Revisit everything you saved under your topics.
